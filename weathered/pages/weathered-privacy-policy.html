Weatherd Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the information collected or submitted when using the Weathered app on iOS and other platforms.

Collected Information

Some limited data may be recorded under anonymous identifiers to provide feedback on how users interact with the app, and to detect and resolve issues.

User location, IP addresses, and identifiable data is not collected or recorded. Personally identifiable information is not knowingly shared with third parties.

Users are not tracked and no location data is ever sent to external servers or services other than the third parties detailed in this policy.

iCloud

Application settings and stored locations are saved and synchronised between devices using a users iCloud account associated with an AppleID. Users can disable Weathered access to this functionality in the the settings app of their device.

Third Parties

Weathered provides forecast data from Apple WeatherKit which is subject to the Apple privacy policy.

No other third parties are used.
